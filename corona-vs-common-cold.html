Difference between Corona and Common Cold

A human body is a combination of various systems. These systems work together in alignment to make the body fit and healthy. But sometimes we face some issues with our bodies. Sometimes the systems do not work properly, due to which we might land up in a grave situation. That is why we are always suggested to eat well and exercise because 'health is wealth.'

So, it is all the more essential to take care of oneself. Home has become the safest place for us. Corona Virus has killed lakhs of people in the country. During the first wave, we were mature enough to stay in our houses. But in the second wave, people have become more careless, and this is the reason for so many cases in India. Now, before moving on with the discussion, let us first understand the primary meaning of COVID-19 and Common Cold.

Corona Virus

Corona Virus or COVID-19 is an infectious and contagious disease. This virus has been discovered a year earlier. The effect of the corona virus begins with mild symptoms like fever, common cold, etc. One can take medications and might not need any special treatment. But if a person is experiencing symptoms like a fall in the oxygen level, then he/ she must immediately visit a doctor. COVID-19 spreads through the germs generated by an infected person (a person coughing or sneezing). These germs are inhaled by the other person resulting in the other person being affected. Coronavirus begins with mild symptoms but can turn into severe respiratory disease. Thus, it is advised to visit a doctor as soon as you see the mild symptoms.

Common Cold

Common Cold is defined as the viral infection of the throat and nose. The common cold can be caused due to different types of viruses. Common cold can happen due to a change of weather or being in contact with the infected person.

The common cold is harmless and can be cured with simple medications. One can drink turmeric milk, inhale some steam, or take medicines to cure the cold. The common cold is treatable, and a person can be cured within days to weeks. Now, it is essential to know the significant differences between the common cold and coronavirus. The difference between them is given below.

S.NO. CORONA VIRUS COMMON COLD
1. Corona Virus is an infectious disease that can be passed on from one person through the other through inhalation and exhalation. On the other hand, the common cold is defined as the viral infection of the throat and nose.
2. Corona Virus can be treated if early action is taken, i.e., medications during the mild symptoms. Common cold can be treated through home remedies and medications.
3. The period of incubation of the virus is 2-14 days. The period of incubation of the common cold is 2-4 days.
4. Cough and shortness of breath are common in the coronavirus. Cough and shortness of breath is a mild symptom and is not much seen in the cold.
5. Fever is common in this virus. Fever is a rare condition in the common cold.
6. A person does not have a sore throat or a runny nose. A person having a common cold has a sore throat or a runny.
7. Loss of taste and smell happens in the coronavirus. A person does not feel the loss of taste or smell in the common cold.
8. Corona virus can become a serious condition if the treatment is not done on time. The common cold is not a severe situation, and a person who has a cold can be treated easily.

So, these are some of the significant differences between common cold and coronavirus. The initial phases of both the conditions are different, i.e., coronavirus begins from having fever, while common cold begins from having a runny nose or a sore throat. In both the situation, immediate treatment is required. Now, let us see some of the symptoms of Corona and Common Cold.

Symptoms of Corona Virus

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Difficulty in Breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Pain in the muscles
  • headache
  • Body-ache
  • Nausea
  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhoea

Symptoms of Common Cold

  • Pain in muscles
  • Runny nose
  • Sore Throat
  • Watery Eyes
  • Congestion
  • Sneezing

Coronavirus is becoming life-threatening every single day. Therefore, it is essential to take the preventive measures to combat this virus.

Steps to avoid COVID-19

  1. Mask: Wearing a mask is one of the most important steps that has to be taken against coronavirus. From a two years baby to an older person, everyone is required to wear masks. Certain guidelines have been declared by the government for the same. It is high time that we understand that wearing a mask is very necessary to stay alive.
  2. Maintaining 6 meters distance: A distance of six meters is required between two people so that the virus does not transmit itself. Everyone must maintain a distance among themselves to avoid getting the virus.
  3. Getting vaccinated: Vaccinations are important to combat this virus. The vaccinations serve as a protective layer against the virus. Thus, getting vaccinated must be the priority of every individual.
  4. Washing your hands: Washing your hands time and again is another important factor. Whether you come from outside or do dusting or cleaning of the house, you must wash your hand in order to avoid the transmission of the virus. Viruses can be anywhere, and therefore, one must not take any risks.
  5. Disinfecting & Cleaning: Sanitizers must be there with everyone. It is not possible to wash hands when we are out. Therefore, sanitizers are required to clean out hands. In other cases, homes must also be cleaned with disinfectants so that the virus does not harm anyone in the house.
  6. Yoga & Meditation: Yoga, over the years, has proved to be the cure for many diseases. Yoga and meditation help in increasing the immunity levels in the body that can combat any kind of disease. This is the time to start yoga and meditation in order to increase immunity levels and stay calm in this current situation.
  7. Eating Right: As we know, that the pandemic situation is worse, and it will worsen in the near future. Eating green leafy vegetables and citrus fruits is important to keep ourselves healthy and fit.

So, these are some of the major steps that people must imbibe to keep their surroundings healthy. The same preventive measures like eating right, exercising, yoga, and meditation can be undertaken in the common cold as well. But since common cold is not that of a grave situation, so it can be cured by taking medications for two days. The common cold is one of the symptoms of COVID-19. Therefore, several precautions must be taken. As soon as one feels some symptoms like cold or fever, medications must be taken immediately to avoid further damage to the system. Thus, both Corona Virus and Common Cold can be treated when the medications and treatment are undertaken immediately.
