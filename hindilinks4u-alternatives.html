HindiLinks4u Alternatives

What is HindiLinks4u?

HindiLinks4u is an entertainment website that contains several Bollywood, south Indian, and Hollywood movies. All the movies are available free of cost, and you can download and/ or watch almost all Hindi and English movies on this site. It also contains popular web series and other television shows. You can easily download all kinds of newly released movies and series without paying any charges. It is open to all and is an easy-to-use web-based platform.

Although it has a lot of traffic on its sites, there are some missing features. It does not present descriptions or screenshots of related content. Sometimes it takes a long time to download movies and series. Additionally, let's say you have started downloading a movie, and you want to pause it and resume it later. In that case, the file gets corrupted after half the download, so your data on that file is wasted. There are also many advertisements on every page of this platform, which are annoying at times. So, it may not meet all the needs of every user. However, many alternative platforms are available where you can download and watch all the movies and series.

It is a good platform as it contains all the updated content; the most important thing is that everything is free for all users.

Vumoo will allow you to download and watch all the content you want. If you are a Netflix user, you will notice that this site is almost identical to Netflix and very easy to use accordingly.

Like HindiLinks4u, OFilmyWap is also a high-traffic site. Since content is uploaded regularly, you can get every newly released movie within 2-3 days on this site. It provides high-quality and durable content. You can select the quality of the movies and series before downloading them. The highest quality is 1080p available on this site, and the lowest quality is 360p. You can choose according to your preference.

One drawback of this platform is that it contains some heavy advertisements while downloading the content, so it may sometimes be distracting. Still, it is the perfect platform, and all the entertaining content is available here.

Gomovies is a very convenient and excellent alternative to HindiLinks4u. It is also a free and easy-to-use platform that consists of several films and series. If you are a daily user of the HindiLinks4u site, then you will find this site/platform more helpful as the user interface of the Gomovies site is similar to or even better than HindiLinks4u in some aspects. YesMovies is also a fantastic movie-watching platform, similar to other entertaining sites. It includes several movies and series in many languages. FilmyZilla is another trending platform for downloading movies and similar content. It is mainly seen as the competitor of the OFilmyWap as it looks almost similar. FilmyZilla provides updated content regularly, so you can download new series and movies here at zero cost. It gives every type of television series, anime series, and even web series that are present on Netflix, Amazon Prime, and other paid OTT (over-the-top) platforms. Although it is a high-traffic website, you won't find any issues with the browsing speed and page responses while navigating through the platform. One drawback of this site is that it shows many ads when you visit it, and hence the website lags on mid or low-end devices. With FilmyZilla, you can easily download movies and series in high-resolution quality.

ShowBox is also recommended for watching movies and series and is considered an alternative to HindiLinks4u. Here, you get all types of entertaining content that you need. LookMovie is a trending and growing web-based entertainment platform. It consists of an excellent user interface or design and is easy to use. You can find all types of movies and series on this site, and you can easily stream all the desired content with the higher-resolution quality. One of the best things about LookMovie is that it provides the IMDb rating of each movie as well as the number of reviews and duration of the movie.

Another best thing about LookMovie is that if you want to watch a movie or any series on its website, all you have to do is click on the respective link of the movie or series, and it will start streaming without redirecting you to another server or page. This is for you if you want to view a movie on a single website rather than clicking on many links.

12. MoviesJoy

MoviesJoy is also one of the free movie streaming sites. It also works the same as HindiLinks4u, but there is one additional feature in this platform. It provides an option of 'Most Searched/ Watched Movies' to all the users who want to find good movies. With this approach, MoviesJoy allows you to view films that have been viewed the most on its site. The movie stream categories are updated regularly, and all new releases are displayed on the site.

The site offers a unique feature in which you can search for movies by country and region, allowing you to look for movies from certain regions/ countries. This feature mainly arranges titles by country of origin.

13. VegaMovies

VegaMovies is another best site and somewhat better than the HindiLinks4u site in some aspects. It provides all types of entertaining content like all types of Movies and TV series, including Bollywood, Hollywood, south-Indian, and Korean. Also, apart from movies, it offers paid content from various OTT platforms for free. Therefore, VegaMovies is the best site for OTT platform lovers.

One significant advantage of VegaMovies is that you can also download all the OTT content free of cost. Most of the content on VegaMovies is available with high quality, so you can download 4k quality video content as well.

Apart from this, one major drawback of VegaMovies is that it contains some annoying ads. These ads sometimes auto-redirect users to other sponsored sites or platforms that may make it tough for non-tech-savvy people to use the platform. Also, you face a little difficulty while downloading the content, as it usually goes through two or three different pages while generating the final downloading link.

14. 5Movies

5Movies is another easy-to-use HindiLinks4u alternative that has been around for a long time. The site's user interface is straightforward, and navigating is simple. It also provides entertaining content, including all the new/ updated movies and television series. One of the additional features of 5movies is that it allows direct sharing features for Social media sites such as Twitter, Facebook, and Reddit. The site is updated regularly, so check back frequently to find the latest content. The latest additions are displayed on the site's 'Homepage' along with the appropriate collection under the 'Recommendations' section.

YoMovies is another perfect alternative to HindiLinks4u. It is one of the best platforms for watching and downloading all type of entertaining content, and it is the best site for those users who loves to watch Bollywood and Hindi movies. The online movies section consists of all kinds of Bollywood, new and old movies, free of cost. You can watch or download them here without any login/sign-up process.
