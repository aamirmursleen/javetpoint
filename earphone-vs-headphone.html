Difference between Earphone and Headphone

Headphones and earphones are used for listening to audio. They are commonly used nowadays as they offer lots of benefits. They provide privacy to the user, which means that surrounding people are not able to hear the audio that the user is listening to. They are used for listing songs, speaking on calls, while watching videos, etc. The quality of audio and other functionalities may vary company-wise or brand-wise. Besides this, both earphone and headphone can be used while doing work, jogging, walking, playing, and while performing any type of activities.

Earphone

It is used for listening to audio. It has two speakers and one mic. They are worn by inserting slightly into ear but they cannot cover the whole ear. They are small in size. They are light in weight means their weight is less and easy to carry. Earphones have silicon's rubbers that provide comfort to users. The features of earphones are different according to brand. The brand also offers different colors of earphones.

While using the earphone, it cannot cut the surrounding noise, due to which a person cannot hear clearly. So, he may increase the volume to hear clearly, but, high volume is not good for ears.

Types of Earphones

There are two types of earphones that are wired and wireless earphones.

1. Wireless earphones:

The earphones which are not connected by wire to the device are called wireless earphones. They are connected to the device via Bluetooth. If the range of the Bluetooth is high, the user can place the device or move anywhere within Bluetooth range, so it does not require the user to carry the device everywhere. These earphones are light-weight and also easy to carry. They can have following two types;

  • Ear pods:
    It is also called earbuds. It has two small speakers who are not connected with each other with wire; they are separated. The user wears it in-ear. They are very small in size, so it's easy to carry them. These are comfortable to use. It needs charging for working. The charging case is used to charge the ear pods. The charging case comes with the Air Pods. It is connected to the device through Bluetooth. In that, the mic is provided for speaking purposes. The pause and autoplay functionality are available. Some ear pods are waterproof. Apple, Bose, RHA, Sony, boat, etc., are brands of ear pods.
  • Neckband earphones:
    Its two speakers are connected through a wire. The user wears the speaker in-ear. When the user uses it, the wire comes from the back of the user's neck; that's why it is called neckband earphones. It is connected to the device through Bluetooth. They are lightweight and easy to carry. These are comfortable to use. It needs charging for working. The charging slot is present on it for charging. The pause and autoplay functionality are available in this. It has a mic also. Every brand has a different price for neckband earphones. Boat, One plus, Sony, JBL, realme, Boult, bang and Olufsen, Jabra, bose, etc., are brands of neckband earphones.

2. Wired earphones:

These earphones are connected to the device through the wire that's why they are called wired earphones. They are worn in the ear. These earphones do not need charging for work. The pause and autoplay functionality are available in it. The earphone cord is connected to the device, so the user has to handle the device carefully. Every brand has a different price for wired earphones. Some popular brands of wired earphones are Boat, One plus, Sony, JBL, realme, etc.

Headphones

It is used for listening to audio. It also comes with two speakers and one mic. Headphones are big in size and has soft cushions that provide comfort to users. The rounded shape fiber belt is called a headband that comes overhead. Some headphones provide an adjustable head belt. They are difficult to carry because they occupy more space and are not light-weight.

The functionality of headphone may vary according to the brand; some headphone has SD card slot, FM, playing button, etc., and some headphones do not contain this features. The user can buy headphones of his favorite color as brands offer the headphones in different colors like back, blue, white, etc.

For the health, the headphone is thousand times better than earphones. While using the headphone, it cuts the surrounding noise. The person can hear clearly at low volume also, and there is no need the increase the volume to hear clearly. They also cover the whole ear as they are big in size.

Types of headphones:

There are two types of headphones that are wired and wireless headphones.

1. Wireless headphones:

They are connected to the device through Bluetooth. The speaker is big, and these two speakers are connected by the fiber or plastic's rounded belt. They are a little bit heavyweight. The user wears it on-ear, and the fiber or plastic belt comes on the head. It needs charging for working. A charging slot is available for charging, by using adaptor and cord user charge it. It tells playback time in hours after full charging. The pause and autoplay functionality are available in this phone. Some headphones provide the slot for sd cards, on-off buttons, and FM also. It has mic also. The popular brands of headphones are boat, infinity, leaf bass, Garbo labs, etc.

2. Wired headphones:

These headphones are connected with the device through the wire. The headphone cord is connected to the device, so the user has to handle the device with care. There is no need to charge it. It also has a mic. The price of it varies according to the brand. The brands of wired headphones are boat, hp, Sony, JBL, Phillips, abyss, bose, faro, Hosa Technology, etc.

Side effects of headphones and earphones:

The side effect of it is hearing loss or patients may start to feel some wired sound in the ear. When two-three people speaking at a time, then the patient can not be able to hear everyone. They also disturb the normal aeration of the ear canal.

To avoid any harmful effects of headphones or earphones on the body, keep the sound below 85 decibels and more than 25 decibels and use the headphone and earphone, which has a good quality speaker. Because sound above 85 decibels is harmful to the ear, and less than 25 decibels are unhealthy for the ear.
